Day 9
June 4, 2013
Funeral of Abdullah Cömert (22) held with thousands of attendees in Hatay, Turkey. Vice Prime Minister Bülent Arınç had a meeting with the President Abdullah Gül, then announced that the negotiations is expected to begin with the protestors tomorrow.
Taksim was peaceful during the whole day. However, the clashes kept going in Beşiktaş and severely proliferated in Tunceli and Antakya at the night.
